# 📊 E-commerce Sales Dashboard

## Business Problem
A retail store needed to identify which products, 
regions, and time periods were driving revenue 
and profit.

## What I Did
- Cleaned 9,994 rows of sales data in Excel
- Added Profit Margin calculated column
- Built an interactive Power BI dashboard 
  with 7 visuals

## Key Insights
- Technology leads sales at $836K (36% of total)
- Q4 consistently shows highest sales every year
- West region drives 31% of total revenue

## Tools Used
Excel · Power BI

## Dataset
Superstore Sales Dataset — Kaggle (public)
